> how verification works

  • 30 binary criteria across 5 levels (L1 to L5)
  • each level has 6 criteria. need 4 out of 6 to pass a level
  • levels are cumulative - can't be L3 without passing L1 and L2
  • your final level is the highest level you fully pass
  • once verified, you get a badge you can embed on your site
